📱 AdiGuide

A mobile app for self-guided walks and city exploration across Russia.
Listen to engaging audio stories, explore at your own pace, and discover the hidden gems around you.

Why AdiGuide?

  • 🎧 Audio format — no need to read, just listen
  • 🗺️ Curated routes and selections
  • 📍 Works offline — no internet required
  • 🇷🇺 Built for domestic travel in Russia

How it works

Open the app, choose a city and a route — and start walking.

The app automatically plays the right audio guide as you move along.

You can also listen like a podcast — in your headphones or on speaker.

Who it's for

  • 🌍 Travelers exploring Russia
  • 🏙️ Locals who want to rediscover their own city
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Parents with kids
  • 📚 School and university students
  • 😴 Anyone tired of boring tours
