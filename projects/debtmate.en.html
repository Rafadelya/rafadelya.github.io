💸 DebtMate

A simple and reliable debt manager — fair, fast, and hassle-free.
Keep track of debts between friends and acquaintances offline, with no registration and no unnecessary complexity.

Why DebtMate?

  • 💰 Tracks who owes what and to whom
  • 🤝 Supports group debts and shared expenses
  • 🔐 Local data storage — secure and private
  • 📱 Cross-platform: iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, Linux
  • 💬 Friendly reminders without being annoying
  • 📊 Full history of debts and repayments always at hand

How it works

  • Add friends and debts, create groups — for example, for shared trips or living arrangements.
  • DebtMate calculates the total balance and shows exactly who needs to repay whom.
  • Everything is stored locally on your device — no accounts, no cloud.

Who it's for

  • Friends who regularly split expenses
  • Coworkers
  • Roommates
  • Families and travel groups
  • Anyone tired of keeping track manually
