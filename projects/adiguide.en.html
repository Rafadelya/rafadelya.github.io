📱 AdiGuide

A mobile app for self-guided walks and city explorations across Russia.
Listen to engaging audio stories, explore at your own pace, and discover new places nearby.

Why AdiGuide?

  • 🎧 Audio format – no need to read, just listen
  • 🗺️ Convenient routes and curated collections
  • 📶 Works offline
  • 🇷🇺 Made for domestic tourism in Russia

How it works

Open the app, choose a city and a route — and start your walk.

The app automatically plays the audio guide as you move along the path.

You can also listen like a podcast — through headphones or speaker.

Who it's for

  • Travelers exploring Russia
  • City residents who want to learn more about their hometown
  • Parents with children
  • School and university students
  • Anyone tired of boring traditional tours
